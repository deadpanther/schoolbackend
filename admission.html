Admission Process:

Overview:

Admissions are granted in Harrison SCHOOLS to students based on their successful clearing of the admissions aptitude tests and parental interaction from class 1 upwards and a parental interaction for Pre-Nursery and KG and meeting the admissions criteria. Admission Procedure
Stage 1: Submit the online Harrison Schools Enquiry Form from this website or visit the school admissions office.
Stage 2: Harrison School counsellor will get back to you to answer your questions and collect the required information
Stage 3: Parents / Guardian will visit school campus to fill in the admission form
Stage 4: Harrison Schools Admissions department would inform you of the date for parental interaction or entrance test date, whatever is applicable
Stage 5: Student appears for parental interaction for Nursery. Aptitude tests for KG classes and above.
Stage 6: Student Registration & Payment of fees (to be done within 7 working days from test result)
